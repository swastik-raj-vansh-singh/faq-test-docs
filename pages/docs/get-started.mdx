# Get Started

This guide helps you get started.

## Prerequisites

- A Lamatic account

## Deploy

1. Open the Flow in the Editor and click Deploy.
2. Copy the workflow ID from the Connect panel.


## Frequently Asked Questions

### What do I need before I can deploy a Flow?

You need a Lamatic account.

### How do I deploy my Flow?

Open the Flow in the Editor and click Deploy.

### Where do I find the Workflow ID?

The Workflow ID is located in the Connect panel.
